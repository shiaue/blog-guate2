Phase 4: Next Steps

How can we further support / serve this community?
With a software and mechanical engineer leading this trip, how can we utilize the unique skillsets that come with engineering?
Do we need more teachers / education professionals?
Do we need more support in Guatemala?

Education Goals

  • Increase student motivation, develop an appetite for learning.
  • Provide more ways to access educational resources
  • Expose students to different career options
  • Better support the NPO and teachers

Current State of Patzún, Guatemala

NUFED (Escuela de Alternancia)

The school that we observed and worked with in Phase 3.
Students are ages 12-18. There are ~50 students at this school. Currently there are  3 "grades" being taught there.
There are 2 groups of students at this school. The first group consists of two grades and the second group consists of one grade. While the first group is in class, the other group is working a job and vice versa. Each month, both groups will work for half the month and attend classes for the other half.
The 4 full-time teachers and volunteers keep this school going. The whole team is passionate about bringing education to these students. From observation, the curriculum could really be revamped to better target the learning style and ability of the students. With a shortage of staff, the school didn't even seem to have a curriculum or lesson plan, tests were a group activity, and curiosity had limited space to roam free.

Fundated

This is the NPO that we partnered with and connected us to NUFED
There is a lot of potential with this partnership. Our main contact is a gritty, kind, passionate, intelligent hard worker. She is willing to listen and collaborate with us to continue supporting the education of these students. This is also the most promising way to ensure that work we do there can be sustained and continued. There is a physical building and a team of 5 + some volunteers year round.

Asociación Vida Clinic

The clinic that I first taught tech classes in. (Phase 2) This clinic also operates a pharmacy and a water treatment plant in the same building.
This relationship is not the most stable at the moment. The main doctor and Willow Creek Church have halted their partnership (Willow Creek connected me with them to begin with). This clinic has a lot of influence in Patzún and could be useful to work with them in the future to promote our educational initiatives. They have worked with several American volunteers in the past. This could be an unique internship option for students (in Patzun) to learn about medicine, pharmacy,  business, accounting, or water treatment.

Peace Corps (in Patzun)

There are multiple Peace Corps initiatives ongoing in Patzun.
We have contacts on the education and medical projects in Patzún. Given that these projects are usually ~2 years, these contacts would be able help continue our efforts. (Especially if they intersect with the projects that they are doing as well)

What's Next? (Ideas)

FUNDATED Computer Lab / Maker Space

The idea is to build a computer lab in FUNDATED for students who want to continue learning on their own time or work on homework with internet resources.
Incentives for students could be implemented. (e.g. earning computer time with projects, interning as a lab manager to manage the lab)
Internet based learning is an option (depending on internet connectivity there)

Resources Required:

  • Laptops (we already have enough to build a lab)
  • Peripherals (mice, keyboards)
  • Power Strips
  • Tables
  • Chairs
  • Internet?
  • Local server (host files and connect all laptops)

Scholarship Program (Financial Support)

Creating a scholarship program to incentivise students to work hard. Give them the opportunity to apply and prepare for the program. Potentially send them to Guatemala City to intern with another organization.
Even if we had donors, we don't have a financial plan. How can we use financial resources to better support FUNDATED, which would then be able to better support the surrounding schools?

Teacher Workshops / Resources

Leveling up the teachers at this school is a priority. This could be in the form of workshops, preparing curriculums for them, or something else. We provided laptops to the teachers last trip and hopefully those have been easy to integrate into their lives and workflows.
