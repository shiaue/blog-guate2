Guatemala Phase 1

Objective:

  1. FTK - (for the kids / for the kingdom) Lead the high schoolers well. These week long trips are really for the hearts of the students. I took a lot from these trips when I went in high school and I want to do my best so they can do the same.
  2. Information gathering - See where I can help in Phase 2. Assess the technology they have and the current status of the students and resources there. Assess if Phase 2 is feasible and if there is willingness to learn computer science / technology related content.

Post Trip Review (May 2018)

  1. The students definitely connected well. I love them all ❤️
  2. So many bonding experiences from the trip. Wow... 🙌
  3. The "skit" was an absolute flop 😂... no one  knew what was happening. We didn't make it in time to attend the church service so by the time we got there we just performed our skit and left.
  4. We "purelled" our hands so much so, that e.coli seemed like a myth.
  5. Knee is recuperating well.
  6. There indeed is a need for me to return and help with my technology passion and experience! There really aren't any computer science opportunities in Patzun.
